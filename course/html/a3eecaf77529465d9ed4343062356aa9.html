We hope you launched your own Theia container and saw Virtual Labs in action! To add some context, we'll now look at how some of our customers use Virtual Labs in the real world, with actual learners. The main use-cases tend to be:

  • Software providers give learners hands-on experience with their tools, to drive sales and marketing, allowing users to "try before you buy"
  • Software companies provide experiential learning to widen industry adoption of their solution
  • Software providers enable tech conference/event attendees to test-drive their tools live on the conference room, using any computer with a web browser (via Virtual Labs Live Events, which we cover later in this unit.)
  • Course authors train learners to complete exercises in open source software to learn their subject (such as data science, machine learning and others)

For more on this, can also check out our blog post on 6 Software Training Use Cases for Virtual Training Labs and also 4 Ways Virtual Lab Environments Can Supercharge Your Sales Strategy.
