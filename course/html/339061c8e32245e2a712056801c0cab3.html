There's not just tools for learners in the LMS, however! You'll see that you have some buttons down the side of each page - these allow you to quickly view the page you're on in Studio (which we'll be talking about soon).

You'll also see a Staff Debug Info button, that enables you to perform advanced troubleshooting on each component that makes up your pages.

At the top of the page you should also have a dropdown to view the course as a learner, removing all these additional buttons, or jumping into the shoes of a specific learner enrolled in your course. 

You also have an additional tab in the top navigation that learners never get to see - the Instructor Dashboard.
