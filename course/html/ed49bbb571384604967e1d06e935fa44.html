To review from earlier, Tahoe has four main parts:

  • The LMS: Where learners learn stuff and instructors handle learner management
  • Studio: Where courses are built and maintained
  • The Management Console: Where your define your site's look and feel and manage site-wide content
  • Figures: Where you get data about your learners and courses

If you are currently a Trial user, you are on the Tahoe Starter plan. Which means you get every bit of this! Next up, we'll break down each section and tell you which things you can accomplish where.
