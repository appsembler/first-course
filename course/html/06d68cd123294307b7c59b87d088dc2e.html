We will go deeper later in the course, but for now just know that Tahoe is a symphony in four parts:

  • The LMS: Where learners learn stuff and instructors handle learner management
  • Studio: Where courses are built
  • The Management Console: Where your define your site's look and feel and manage site-wide content (certificates, SSO, custom domain)
  • Figures: Where you get data about your learners and courses

These combined make up Tahoe!
