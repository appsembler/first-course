Professional Services are additional paid services that help you more quickly and easily integrate our platform into your environment. Our goal is to help you achieve business goals faster, and with less friction. A number of things could fall into this category. 

It could mean collaborating on technical implementations or integrations like:

  • Single Sign On (including social logins)
  • API integration (with CMS, Salesforce, etc.)
  • Ecommerce integration (Shopify)
  • Business Intelligence Connector integrations (via Segment to Marketo, Salesforce, etc)
  • Course content migration
  • Container/Docker consulting (for Virtual Labs customers)

Or it could mean more user-centric or design-related services like:

    • Instructional design
    • Advanced user training
    • Custom site themes and branding 

Note: Professional Services are only available to Tahoe Premium customers.
