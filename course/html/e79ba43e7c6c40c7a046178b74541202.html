Welcome to the Appsembler sample course!

This course serves multiple purposes:

  • Share a bit about Appsembler
  • Demo a finished course built with Tahoe and hosted on Open edX
  • Give you something to get started with to avoid the dreaded 'blank page syndrome'
  • Provide tips and insights into how we built this course (#soMeta)
  • Showcase our hand-on Virtual Labs tool and our premium Tahoe features

We suggest going through the course once on your live site (just hit View Live in the top right if you're in Studio!) to get a feel for how a course can be structured and what you can include. Once you're done, you can open the course in Studio to add your own sections, remove sections, and make any changes you like. It's meant to be your playground for editing, tweaking, and learning!
