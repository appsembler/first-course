Redis Labs, ranked as a leader in NoSQL, in-memory databases, operational databases, and database-as-a-service (DBaaS), is trusted by more than 7,400 enterprise customers. Their software training platform is hosted on Tahoe, as Redis University. 

Redis University makes use of the two main features of Virtual Labs. They offer asynchronous, self-paced training for new and advanced users of their software. And they use our Live Events tool to power learning at events like RedisConf.

In previous years, Redis tried to provide hand-on learning on attendee's machines. But the setup process for a large class of learners was a major logistical and technical challenge. They were experiencing times where they had to give up on the hands-on sessions.

Virtual Labs solved this, providing a very different, seamless experience for learners. Users just launched a browser, logged into a virtual lab, and instantly were using Redis! Thanks to Virtual Labs, hundreds of conference attendees used were able to launch labs and take part in hands-on training exercises on the conference floor.
