About This Course

This sample course is included with every Appsembler Tahoe trial. Inside this course we:

  • Teach you the basics of Tahoe and Open edX
  • Share a bit about Appsembler
  • Demo a finished course - built with Tahoe and hosted on Open edX
  • Provide tips and insights into how we built this course (#soMeta)
  • Share a few customer success stories
  • Showcase our hands-on Virtual Labs tool and our premium Tahoe features

We suggest going through the course once to get a feel for how a course can be structured and what you can include. After that, feel free to add your own sections, remove sections, and make any changes you like. It's meant to be your playground for editing, tweaking, and learning!
